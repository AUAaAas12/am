Amwal Financial Consultants is a Saudi Limited Liability Company that operate Under the Capital Market Low.

Amwal is licensed by (CMA) to provide financial advisory services (under license number 30/23060).

Amwal renders its financial advisory services that comply with Islamic Sharia’a to different business groups, individuals, establishments and companies in Saudi Arabia.

Today, Amwal operated in Saudi Arabia through its head office in Riyadh and its regional office in Dammam.

Amwal is also capable of extending its services to clients in other parts of the Middle East through its affiliated/ sister companies located in the Qatar and Egypt.
